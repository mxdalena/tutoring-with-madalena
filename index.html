About Me

I am a full-time mathematics tutor working with

students at a variety of levels: primary, GCSE,

A-Level and university. I graduated from King's

BSc Mathematics.

My Mission

My passion is to guide growing brilliant minds to reach

a level of confidence that allows them to face any problem

with a clear mind and a range of mathematical tools. I have

my own students, who I teach one-to-one, and I also work for

two agencies: Parallel Academy and Tutorberry. At Parallel I

tutor small classes of gifted students to prepare for Maths

Challenges and at Tutorberry I am part of a growing team of

female maths tutors.
